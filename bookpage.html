L.K. West

Psychological Suspense Author

L. k. West is a private observer of human behavior who writes at the intersection of power, secrecy, and consequence. Drawing inspiration from real situations and lived tension, West explores how silence forms, how desire distorts, and how easily truth becomes collateral damage. A former participant and close observer of faith spaces, West writes with an insider’s awareness and an outsider’s restraint. If They Find Out is part of a growing body of work that examines what happens when intimacy, belief, and control collide. West writes to amplify real stories often left unnamed.
